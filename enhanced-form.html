Top Travel Destinations in Kenya

  • Maasai Mara: World-famous for the Great Migration and Big Five safaris.
  • Diani Beach: White sand, turquoise waters, and relaxed coastal vibes.
  • Mount Kenya: Africa’s second-highest peak with alpine beauty and hiking trails.
  • Lake Nakuru: A paradise for birdwatchers and home to rhinos and flamingos.
